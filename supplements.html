03 — Blood flow
L-Citrulline
Nitric oxide and erectile function
L-Citrulline converts to L-Arginine in the body, which increases nitric oxide production. Nitric oxide is the mechanism behind erectile function — it relaxes blood vessels and allows blood flow. A 2011 pilot study found meaningful improvement in mild ED with L-Citrulline supplementation alone. 3–6g daily. More bioavailable than L-Arginine directly.

04 — Sleep quality
Magnesium Glycinate
Deep sleep and testosterone
Sleep quality correlates more strongly with PIED recovery speed than almost any other lifestyle factor — 70–80% of testosterone production occurs during deep sleep. Magnesium glycinate improves sleep quality without sedation or grogginess. Take 400mg before bed. Most men notice a real difference in sleep depth within two weeks. The glycinate form is well-absorbed and gentle on the stomach. Improving sleep is one of the most underrated parts of the porn quitting process →
