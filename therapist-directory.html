What to look for

HOW TO CHOOSE
THE RIGHT THERAPIST.

🎯
Clear specialism
Look for a therapist who explicitly lists pornography addiction, compulsive sexual behaviour, or sexual compulsivity — not one who has listed "sex" as one of 30 interests.
🤝
No shame approach
The right therapist approaches this as a behavioural pattern, not a moral failing. If you feel judged in the first session, find someone else. Not sure yet if you have a problem? See the signs of porn addiction →
💻
Online is fine
Many men find online therapy easier for this topic — it reduces the barrier of walking into an office. Online availability means you are not limited to your immediate area. For men who want peer support rather than clinical therapy, one-on-one coaching is an alternative starting point.
