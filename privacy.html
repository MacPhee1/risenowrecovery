Overview

Our Approach to Privacy

Risenow was built for men dealing with something deeply personal. The anonymity is not incidental — it is the reason many men feel safe enough to start. We designed every part of this platform around that reality.

This policy applies to risenowrecovery.com, the Risenow app, and all associated services. It tells you plainly what information we collect, how it is used, and what your rights are.

"We built this so a man can use Risenow for six months, post regularly in the community, and never once reveal who he actually is. That is intentional and it will not change."

03
How Data Is Used

What We Do With What We Collect

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve Risenow. We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertisers. We do not use your information to build a profile of you as an individual.

Community posts

Posts made in the Risenow community are visible to other community members under your chosen username. They are not indexed by search engines. They are not shared with third parties. They remain associated with your username unless you choose to delete them.

Email address (Pro subscribers)

Your email address is used by Stripe to process and manage your subscription. We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Stripe but do not receive your full card details. Your email is never connected to your community username and is never visible to other users.

If you opt in to our newsletter separately, your email is stored in ConvertKit and used only to send the content you subscribed to. You can unsubscribe at any time from any email we send.

Analytics

We use anonymised aggregate data to understand which features are used most, where men tend to drop off, and how to improve the experience. This data cannot be traced to an individual user.

05
Your Rights

What You Can Ask Us to Do

You have the following rights regarding any personal data we hold about you.

Access

You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you. For most users this will be minimal — a community username and, for Pro subscribers, a Stripe billing record.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your community account and all associated posts at any time. For Pro subscribers, cancelling your subscription also removes your billing record from our systems within 30 days.

Correction

If any data we hold about you is inaccurate, you can request that it be corrected.

Portability

You can request your data in a portable format at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We respond to all requests within 14 days.

06
Cookies

Cookies and Tracking

The risenowrecovery.com website uses a minimal number of cookies necessary for the site to function — session management and security. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

The Risenow app stores streak, journal, and rank data locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data does not leave your device unless you choose to share it in the community.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data for risenowrecovery.com.

Users Under 18

Risenow Recovery is designed for adult users. The risenowrecovery.com app and community are intended for men aged 18 and over.

Before You Click — our separate publication available at risenowrecovery.com — is written for young men aged 12 to 17 and contains no explicit content. That publication does not involve any data collection from young readers.

If you believe a user under 18 has created a community account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.
